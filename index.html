I feel tired no matter how much I sleep 😔
🧠 Understanding
Excessive tiredness despite adequate sleep often points to poor sleep quality, not duration — light cycles, stress hormones, or blood sugar may be disrupting your deep sleep stages.
💡 Clear Advice
Focus on sleep quality over quantity. Cool your room to 18°C, eliminate blue light an hour before bed, and stabilize blood sugar with a protein-rich dinner.
✅ Action Steps
1
Set a consistent sleep and wake time — even weekends
2
No screens 60 minutes before bed tonight
3
Try 4-7-8 breathing to fall asleep faster
